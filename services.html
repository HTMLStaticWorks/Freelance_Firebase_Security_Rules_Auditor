How It Works

A transparent, step-by-step methodology ensuring zero disruption to your active users.

1

Discovery

Reviewing your app's functionality, data schema, and current ruleset in a staging environment.

2

Simulation

Running automated penetration tests locally using the Firebase Emulator Suite to map vulnerabilities.

3

Development

Drafting the new ruleset offline, creating robust custom validation functions, and testing extensively.

4

Deployment

Providing the exact code to paste into your console or CI/CD pipeline, alongside a full mitigation report.
