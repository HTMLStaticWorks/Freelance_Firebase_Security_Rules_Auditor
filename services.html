Beyond the Automated Scanner

Many developers rely on automated "security checklists" or basic CLI scanners. While these can catch low-hanging fruit, they are fundamentally unable to understand your application's unique business logic. A scanner won't know that a user should only be able to edit their own profile, or that a "manager" role shouldn't be able to delete "admin" documents.

My manual auditing process goes deeper. I map out your entire data schema and simulate complex user interactions to identify logical bypasses that could lead to privilege escalation or mass data extraction. In Firebase, security is logic, and logic requires a human expert.
