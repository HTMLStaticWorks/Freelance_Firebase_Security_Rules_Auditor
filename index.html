Stop Attack Vectors Dead in Their Tracks

Most Firebase breaches happen due to simple misconfigurations. I protect against the most common and devastating attacks.

  • Privilege Escalation

    Preventing users from upgrading their own roles to 'admin' by strictly validating incoming data fields.

  • Mass Data Extraction

    Implementing query limits and list access restrictions to stop scraping bots from downloading your entire database.

  • Data Corruption

    Ensuring that only correctly typed and formatted data can be written to specific collections.
